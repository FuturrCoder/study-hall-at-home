About Study Hall at Home

Made by Mirai DTN, '24 (mnishioka@commschool.org)

Study Hall at Home is a website where Commonwealth students can create and join Zoom rooms to study specific topics.

To use it, first click to sign in with your commschool.org email.

Then, a
  • Rooms
button should show up in the menu bar.

When you click the button, you will be brought to the Rooms page, where you can see all user-created rooms. This icon person shows that a room link has been visited in the past hour. You also have the option to create a new room by clicking the button. When you click the button, you will see a modal, where you can enter the topic of the meeting and in how many hours the room will expire (or choose to make it permanent). To create the room, click the button. Creating a room can take up to 20 seconds. Once the room is created, the page will reload and you will be able to see the link on the Rooms page.
